shine

Seeking the peace within the pieces
Left by the beast,
The screams that call out the abyss
Devouring the fleece
Making me seek more layers while searching for self.
Capturing souls for one in whom I care
As I take in a city to travel a course
Before I get divorced from New York
I'd like to gain one thought or a line
To whisper in your ear
the day I turn back to sunshine.
