Love


The heart spins on words left in frame
That makes the inner soul lament
Wondering
Could they mean more? afraid to ask
Afraid to pass on clues that they
mean more to you...Are we children
to love? Scratching back toward
the womb or lions waiting inside
the den? Open your mouth
or only air will get in

Lynn Gentry
August 23, 2014
Bedford Ave. & N 6th Street, Brooklyn
